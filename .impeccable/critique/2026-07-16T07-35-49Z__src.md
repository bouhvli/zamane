# Minor Observations

- Undoable delete commits (not cancels) on unmount within the 5s window — consider flushing as cancelled or holding the toast across navigation.
- Card padding conventions coexist (gap-6 default vs gap-0 p-4) — pick one.
- Quick-amount chips are labeled +$10…+$100 but set rather than add — the "+" implies additive.

# Questions to Consider

- The Home hero is the one licensed loud surface — is a glass-chip metrics grid the most couples-feeling thing to put there, vs. avatars / a shared streak?
- Whole-goal delete is the one non-undoable action in an otherwise reversible system — is a modal confirm safer than a 10s undo for shared financial history?
- Every detail page leads with a form and buries the content — should the content lead and the add-form become a thumb-zone action?
